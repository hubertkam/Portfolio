Filament Spool Re-Wind Machine

Mechanical Design & MATLAB Gear Analysis

1 / 5

PROJECT INFO

Year 2025
Role Mechanical Systems Designer
Tools SolidWorks, MATLAB

PROJECT SUMMARY

Objective

Design a desktop machine to transfer third-party filament onto proprietary 3D-printer spools while maintaining controlled tension.

Tools / Software

SolidWorks

MATLAB

Method

A 25 W motor with a 120:1 gear reduction was selected to provide low speed and high torque.

The mechanical layout was designed to maintain consistent filament tension during winding.

Gear bending strength was analysed in MATLAB using the Lewis bending equation for PMMA and PLA gears.

Key Results

PMMA gear bending capacity ≈ 63.4 N·m

PLA gear bending capacity ≈ 52.9 N·m

PMMA showed ~20% higher bending strength for identical geometry.

Conclusion

PMMA was the stronger material for this application.

The final design provides consistent winding and sufficient torque for desktop operation.
