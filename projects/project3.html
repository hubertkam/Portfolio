Personal Project

Formula Student Tubular Space Frame Chassis

CAD & FEA Engineering Analysis

1 / 2

PROJECT INFO

Year 2025
Role CAD & FEA Engineer
Tools SolidWorks, Abaqus

PROJECT SUMMARY

Modelled an existing Formula Student tubular space-frame chassis in SolidWorks and ran a basic structural check in Abaqus. The aim was not full optimisation, but to verify torsional rigidity and confirm the design was structurally reasonable.

Design & Modelling

Rebuilt the chassis geometry from a reference design in SolidWorks with correct tube sizes and joint layouts. Cleaned up geometry and fixed unrealistic connections that would distort FEA results.

Analysis

Applied a simplified torsional load case in Abaqus to estimate chassis stiffness. Checked torsional rigidity against typical Formula Student ranges (~1,500–3,000 Nm/deg) to ensure results were realistic. Verified stresses stayed below yield and that no members showed obvious failure or numerical artefacts.

Iteration

Identified weak or overstressed areas, made minor geometry changes, and re-ran the analysis to confirm acceptable stiffness and stress levels.

Outcome

Confirmed the chassis was structurally acceptable under simplified torsional loading. Demonstrated correct CAD-to-FEA workflow and the ability to sanity-check results against known engineering benchmarks.
