Personal Project

Differential Gearbox Motion Analysis

CAD Design & Motion Study

1 / 1

PROJECT INFO

Year 2025
Role CAD Design & Motion Analysis
Tools SolidWorks

PROJECT SUMMARY

Designed and assembled a differential gearbox in SolidWorks and carried out a basic motion study to verify correct kinematics. The project focused on confirming gear relationships, rotational directions, and speed ratios rather than detailed stress or durability analysis.

Design & Modelling

Created individual gearbox components and assembled them with correct mates and constraints. Ensured gear geometry, alignment, and clearances were consistent with standard differential layouts.

Motion Analysis

Set up a SolidWorks motion study to observe input–output behaviour of the differential. Verified correct power split, relative wheel speeds during straight-line motion and differential action under simulated speed differences.

Outcome

Confirmed correct functional behaviour of the differential gearbox with no interference or kinematic errors. Demonstrated the ability to model mechanical assemblies accurately and use motion analysis to validate system behaviour at an early design stage.
